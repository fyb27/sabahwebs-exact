If you live or work around Jalan Penampang, Taman Fortuna, or Lorong Seroja in Kota Kinabalu, finding a reliable clinic that's open around the clock can make a real difference. Permai Polyclinics Fortuna, located at Fortuna Commercial Centre along Jalan Penampang, is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with no appointment needed.

Where Exactly Is It?

The clinic is situated at Ground Floor, Lot 3, Block D2, Lorong Seroja 2, Mile 2.5, Jalan Penampang, 88200 Kota Kinabalu, Sabah. It's easy to find — right in the Fortuna Commercial Centre, accessible from the main Jalan Penampang road. Parking is available within the commercial centre. ‍🗺️ View on Google Maps

What Services Do They Offer?

Permai Polyclinics Fortuna offers a comprehensive range of general healthcare services, making it a one-stop clinic for individuals and families in the area:

  • General consultation
  • Blood pressure and blood glucose checks
  • Urine and blood tests
  • Vaccination for all ages
  • Health screening and medical check-ups
  • Pre-employment medical examinations
  • Wound dressing and treatment
  • IV drip and injections
  • Nebuliser treatment
  • Ultrasound services
  • Knee injections for osteoarthritis
  • Sick leave and medical certificates
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, cholesterol)
  • Paediatric consultation

Whether you need a routine check-up, treatment for a sudden illness, or a medical certificate for work, the clinic handles it all under one roof.

Open 24 Hours — Walk-Ins Welcome

One of the biggest advantages of Permai Polyclinics Fortuna is its 24-hour operation. Unlike most clinics in Kota Kinabalu that close by evening, this clinic is accessible at any hour — whether it's late at night, early morning, or a public holiday. No appointment is required, just walk in.

Highly Rated by Patients

With over 100 Google reviews and a perfect 5.0 star rating, Permai Polyclinics Fortuna has built a strong reputation among patients in the Jalan Penampang area since opening in early 2026. Patients frequently highlight the clean environment, friendly staff, and short waiting times.
